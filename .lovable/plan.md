

# Website Copy Rewrite: Subscription-Based Service Positioning

## Overview
This plan rewrites all website copy to subtly position Saltarelli Web Studio as an ongoing service provider (Website-as-a-Service) rather than a one-time project-based agency. The messaging shifts from "build and hand over" to "set up and continuously manage" — framing websites, AI agents, and automations as systems that are always being looked after.

---

## Core Messaging Shifts

| Current Language | New Language |
|------------------|--------------|
| "Build your new website" | "Launch your managed website" |
| "Project completion" | "System goes live" |
| "1 round of revisions included" | "Ongoing refinements as your business grows" |
| "Turnaround time" | "Setup time" |
| "Launch & Support" | "Go Live & Ongoing Care" |
| "Domain setup & basic SEO" | "Domain setup, SEO, and continuous optimization" |
| "Your vision to life" | "Your business running smoothly" |
| "Book a Discovery Call" | "Start Your Free Consultation" (in some contexts) |

## Key Principles Applied

1. **Setup vs. Build**: Replaced "build" with "set up" or "launch" to frame the initial work as onboarding, not a finished product

2. **Ongoing Language**: Added words like "continuous," "ongoing," "managed," "monitored," and "always" throughout

3. **Removed Finality**: Eliminated phrases implying project completion or handover

4. **Handled-for-You Tone**: Emphasized that the client doesn't need to worry about tech — it's taken care of

5. **No Explicit Subscription Language**: Avoided overusing "subscription" or "SaaS" — the ongoing nature is implied through service descriptions

6. **Approachable Tone**: Kept language simple and direct for tradesmen and local business owners
